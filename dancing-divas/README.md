# Dancing Divas

An audio-reactive sprite animation that responds to music through your microphone.

## How It Works

Play music near your device and watch the character react to the audio energy - from idle to vibing to full dancing mode.

## Requirements

- Modern browser with microphone access
- Spritesheet files in `/sprites` folder
